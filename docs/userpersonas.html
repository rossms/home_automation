User Personas



Demographic: Middle Class
Background
Age: 20-50
Occupation: Professional 		Main Points
  • Tech-savvy, interested in new technologies
  • Comfortable with computers and computing devices.
  • Economic and financially aware.
  • Interested in internet connected devices and efficient ways for doing things.
Goals
  • Career driven
  • Time efficiency
  • Maximize expenses and savings
Frustration & Pain Points
  • Spends a lot of time online; however, still managing monthly expenses manually and individually.
  • Has no insights into home; therefore, home security is in the back of his mind.
Scenarios
  • Managed monthly expenses through paper.
  • Wastes electricity due to leaving lights on all the time.
  • Worries when leaves home for travel or for work.
 Description
As a working professional, life is lived in a very “Web 2.0” world and technology is common in all aspects of life. Mobility and computing is the preferred method for doing everything such as managing finances, communication through email or IM, transportation, etc.


Demographic: Middle Class
Background
Age: 20-50
Occupation: Professional 		Main Points
  • Family driven
  • Comfortable with computers and computing devices.
  • Economic and financially aware.
  • Interested in using internet connected devices and efficient ways for doing things such as child monitoring or to save money.
Goals
  • Safe environment for family
  • Maximize expenses and savings
  • Not to waste
Frustration & Pain Points
  • Still managing monthly expenses manually and individually.
  • Has no insights into home; therefore, home security is in the back of his mind.
Scenarios
  • Managed monthly expenses through paper.
  • Wastes electricity due to leaving lights on all the time.
  • Worries when leaves home for travel or for work.
  • Children always forgetting to turn off utilities or close doors.
 Description
As the head of household, life is lived in a very structured manner and scheduled. Mobility and computing is the preferred method for doing everything such as managing finances, communication through email or IM, transportation, etc. Always interested in efficiency.


Demographic: Wealthy
Background
Age: 20-50
Occupation: Professional 		Main Points
  • Time sensitive
  • Economic and financially aware.
  • Interested in make life easier, more convenient, and more comfortable
Goals
  • Maximize expenses and savings
  • Not to waste money
  • Make life more easy, convenient, and comfortable.
Frustration & Pain Points
  • Has no insights into home; therefore, home security is in the back of his mind.
  • Doesn’t like wasting money; therefore, wants insights into detailed consumption analytics.
Scenarios
  • Monthly expenses are not detailed enough.
  • Wastes electricity due to leaving lights on all the time.
  • Worries when leaves home for travel or for work.
 Description
As the wealthy professional, life is lived in a very structured manner and scheduled. Mobility and computing is the preferred method for doing everything such as managing finances, communication through email or IM, transportation, etc. Always interested in efficiency and want to insure everything is accessible in real-time.
